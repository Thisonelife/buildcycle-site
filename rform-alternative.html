BuildCycle vs rForm.
The honest version.

rForm earned its place: per-project pricing, unlimited users, years of service. The question is what happens to that bill when you’re busy.

vs flat tier.

SEC. 02  /  Straight answers

Where each
tool wins.

If we only told you our half, you shouldn’t trust any of it.

Pick rForm if…
  • You run exactly one CA project at a time and want to pay for exactly that
  • You want a long-established tool built around Canadian (CCDC) contract workflows
  • A cheap site-report-only project type (CAD $12.95) fits jobs where that’s all you need
Our case
Pick BuildCycle if…
  • Your project count moves. Flat tiers mean a busy year doesn’t multiply your bill
  • Your GCs run Procore — two-way sync for RFIs and submittals on every tier
  • Pay applications with certified owner sign-off and live contract-value rollup
  • Owners and GCs participate by email — no logins to chase
  • Modern, fast, real-time interface your whole team shares free

Flat. Five projects on rForm: ~CAD $200/mo. On BuildCycle: $79. And the bill never climbs mid-season.
