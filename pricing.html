Five questions everyone asks, answered the way we’d want them answered.

Q.01

What counts as an active project?

Any project not archived. Archive finished work anytime to free a slot — archived projects stay fully viewable.

Q.02

What happens when my trial ends?

Day 31 the workspace becomes read-only: everything visible and exportable, nothing editable until you pick a plan. Your data is never deleted.

Q.03

What if I downgrade with too many active projects?

Everything keeps working; you just can’t create new projects until you’re under the new limit.

Q.04

Is the Procore integration extra?

No. Every tier, included.

Q.05

Do my consultants need licenses?

Never. Unlimited users on every tier is the whole point.
